    Gorwing up playing Hockey!

  • I started playing hockey at around 6 years old and ever since then I fell in love with the game and just recently stopped playing competitively.
  • I would say the peak of my career was when I moved away from home after high school to play in Golden British Columbia in the KIJHL league, it was a lot of fun and was an amazing experience and I will cherish all the memories and friends that I have made for life.


  • Currently I only play for fun with my friends at the local outdoor rink or sometimes, indoor public matches that are hosted in Cochrane.
